## Summer Undergraduate Research

### Overview
Participated in undergraduate research focused on embedded systems and real-time operating systems in an aerospace context.

### Contributions
- Researched and worked with Zephyr RTOS for embedded applications  
- Explored flight software architecture and system-level design  
- Gained experience working within a large, open-source engineering project  
- Collaborated with a multidisciplinary team of engineers  

### What I Learned

- The role of RTOS in embedded systems
